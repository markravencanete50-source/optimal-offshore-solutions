▸ 02 // Approach

The pilot, start to scale.

One program, ninety days, targets in writing. Day 90 is a decision point — not a renewal trap.

▸Scope your pilot
01

Scope // 2 weeks

Pick the one program that hurts. We baseline it, agree SLA / CSAT / AHT / FCR targets in writing, and staff a dedicated pod — no blended teams.

02

Run // 90 days

Your pod goes live on your tools and SOPs. Every Friday you get the same scorecard we run internally — hits, misses, and what changes on Monday.

03

Scale // your call

Hit targets: expand seats and scope on the same reporting. Miss them: take the SOPs and walk. Either way, you keep the documentation.
